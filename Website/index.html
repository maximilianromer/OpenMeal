Track your nutrition effortlessly with AI-powered image analysis. Powered by Google Gemini's free models.

All you need is a photo

OpenMeal uses multimodal LLM image analysis to extract accurate nutritional information from just a photo. You can always add a comment for more accuracy.

Get meal insights

OpenMeal estimates the calories, protein, fats, and carb content of each meal. It also provides nuanced advice about the healthy and unhealthy aspects of each meal, so you can make better health decisions.

Track your progress

Hit your goals with an easy-to-use chart feature.

Use top intelligence

Feeling extra hungry today? OpenMeal uses your own Gemini API key, which means that you can log hundreds of meals a day for free.

Before & After

Use the Before & After photo feature to log meals you don't finish with high accuracy. You can also do this with the Fix It button on a meal that has already been logged.
